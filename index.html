Recent Music Performance

This visualisation investigates the most successful artists and songs in recent years, and the possible causes. A random selection of songs from between the year 1998 and 2020 have been selected along with the Billboard end of year artist rankings from 2006 to 2021.


Hover over any data points in any charts to view more information

Properties of Songs

This chart looks at 5 attributes of certain pop, rock and hip hop songs and attempts to discover some trends in the way these songs operate.
  • Danceability The suitability of the song for dancing.
  • Energy: A perceptual measure of the intensity and activity of a song (e.g. high energy song feels fast, loud and noisy).
  • Length: Song length in minutes and seconds
  • Liveness: Probability of being recorded with a live audience.
  • Popularity: A measure of the song's popularity from up to 100
It is interesting to notice in this chart how the different genres relate to danceability, the more danceable songs seem to have lower energy and that there appears to be 3 distinct groups of song length. However, it doesn't appear necessarily as though any of these attributes are specifically linked to the popularity of a song

Potential Sources of Song Popularity

Taking a deeper dive into the relationship between a song's energy and its popularity with additional possible factors relating to genre and liveness.

It appears as though energy still doesn't seem to have much association with popularity and the same can be said for liveness. However, interestingly, on average, rock songs seem to have a higher popularity than those in the pop or hip hop categories.

Top Performing Billboard Artists in Recent Years

In this chart, we analyse the 5 artists who have dominated the Billboard rankings over the last 15 or so years.

Interestingly, all of these 5 artists fall into the pop or hip hop genres, without any rock representatives despite rock seeming to have a higher average popularity in terms of their songs

We can see that over the first few years in our sample, Taylor Swift asolutely dominated the rankings, and while she is still right up the top, Drake has slightly overtaken here in recent years. This may be just one indicator of the increasing popularity in hip hop music.

Top Performing Countries

This map illustrates the countries with the highest performing artists and the relative success of the country compared to its population

North and Central American artists have been quite succesful in the Billboard rankings along with Eastern European artists as well. It is nice to see Australia and New Zealand feature in there as well.

It seems as though country of origin or perhaps continent of origin has something to do with song or artist popularity
